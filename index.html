About Me

I am a fellow developing female artist and I am going to middleschool at the moment. I am into anime and manga, and my art style is based off of those things. My supplies I use is Sakura Pigment fineliners, Sakura white gelly toll pens, and Crayola pencil crayons. Do not underestimate the Crayola's though, because they are actually good pencil crayons for their price. I have 2 dogs and one bird which I love dearly and are with me through my drawing experience. I am still learning new techniques, and still experiencing art styles. I don't do commisions yet, but I am open to requests!
